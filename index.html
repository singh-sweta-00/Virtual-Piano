How It Works

If you don't have a real piano or keyboard at home, you can play this virtual piano from PlayPiano. This online piano keyboard simulator lets you play seamlessly with your computer's keyboard.

This virtual piano gives -

  • play chords with one piano key.
  • different themes for your vibes.
  • easy platform to use.

If you are reading this from a mobile device, rotate it to display the virtual piano in full width.
